How to create a Twitter acount

1) Open twitter in the desktop (twitter.com) or mobile application.

2) Click on Sign Up.

2) Enter your username, your e-mail or your phone and the date of your birth. Then click on next.

3) Accept or denied the twitter accesss to track data to personalize your user experience. Then click on next.

4) Review or change your information. Next click on Sign Up.

5) Verify your account enter a code that Twitter send it to you by e-mail or sms.

5) When you already verify your account, create a password and click on next.

6) Update a profile photo (Optional).

7) Add a Bio (You can skip it).

8) Then select all the categories that you are interested in following (Optional).

9) Finally, select other Twitter accounts that Twitter recommends based on the selected categories (Optional).
